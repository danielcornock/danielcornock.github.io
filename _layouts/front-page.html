Who am I?

Hey, I'm Dan! Welcome to my little corner of the internet. I am a front end developer from Birmingham, United Kingdom, having graduated in Computer Systems Engineering at the University of Birmingham in 2017. Despite the degree, nearly all of my skills in web development are from self-motivated learning!

What do I do?

I enjoy creating tailor-made websites for small businesses. I am a developer first, designer second. However, I spend lots of my time researching design trends and soaking up knowledge that I can use for my projects.

Why choose me?

I am extremely dedicated to getting the job done efficiently, properly, and in good time. I enjoy making people happy, and I won't quit until my clients are 110% satisfied with the product that I have delivered.
