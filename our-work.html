Our Work.

Have a browse through some of the many different types of work that we've completed for our happy customers!

Bespoke cupboards and shelving units

We were set the challenge of designing and creating two identical, bespoke cupboards and shelving units, sized specifically to allow for the height of vinyl records and a record player. The cupboards were constructed and after a fresh coat of paint on the shelves and the entire room, an old study became a rather lovely ‘play’ room which can now be enjoyed by the family.

#bespokeCarpentry

Laminate flooring installation in Redland

12mm laminate flooring was installed throughout a recently converted basement flat in Redland. Almost 800 square feet was laid, and all joints were staggered perfectly, not a millimetre out of place.

#flooring

Sanding & coating an old wooden floor

3 floors in a terraced Victorian house were sanded and coated in between Bishopston and Redland. A finish so smooth you could glide across it.

#flooring

Bespoke wardrobes in a new flat

The developers clearly hadn’t considered the functionality fully before the sale as there was no existing storage space. Ionic builds stepped in and designed and built two enormous bespoke wardrobes, one with a concealed locking system, for the purpose of keeping the contents out of bounds from Airbnber’s!

#bespokeCarpentry

Plumbing & heating installation at Redland Road

Using our great relationships with our network of subcontractors, we installed all new plumbing and heating throughout a recent conversion on Redland road. All pipe work was boxed in and not a wonky radiator pipe in sight throughout 4 floors and over 25 rooms.

#plumbing #heating

Sash windows & double glazing installation

We undertook a complete overhaul of the sash windows in a property on the doorstep of Durdham Downs. The exterior of the windows were in desperate need of refurbishment and the only existing draught excluders were originally duct tape. One of the windows was rotten beyond repair.
We installed all double-glazed units, full draught excluders and decorated each window, with new brass handles and latches, to closely match the existing furnishings throughout the property.
